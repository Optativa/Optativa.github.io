About

The purpose of this site is to host talks and lectures on a variety of topics we're currently researching or learning about. While the focus is primarily on computer science, other subjects are welcome.
Each talk should last between 15 minutes and 30 minutes. If multiple people have shorter presentations (less than 10 minutes), we can group them together into a single session, similar to a series of lightning talks.
Format for submissions:
  • Talk title (with a link to PDF/website, if available)
  • Speaker's name
  • Date
